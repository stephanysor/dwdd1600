Dead by Daylight Vocab

Looping
A Survivor tactic where you run around structures, pallets, and windows in a way that forces the Killer to waste time and makes it harder for them to land a hit.
Hatch
A special escape route that appears when only one Survivor remains alive. Survivors can jump in to escape instantly, but Killers can close it to trigger the Endgame Collapse.
Tunneling
A Killer strategy of focusing on one Survivor repeatedly (especially right after theyâ€™re unhooked) to eliminate them quickly from the match.
Exhaustion
A negative status effect that disables all sprint-related perks (like Sprint Burst, Lithe, or Dead Hard) until the effect ends. Usually caused by using a sprint perk.
Adrenaline
A powerful Survivor perk that instantly heals one health state and gives you a 150% sprint speed boost for 5 seconds when the Exit Gates are powered.
